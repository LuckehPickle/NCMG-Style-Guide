Image Gallery

If all goes as currently planned, the NCMG website will have two separate image galleries. The first of which will be a carousel. Users can click buttons to cycle left and right through the images. After this gallery, we'll have a sort of collage, which allows administrators to add as many images as they want. This article will be discussing the former.
